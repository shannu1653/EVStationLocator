1. What is this EV Station Locator application?
This application helps electric vehicle users easily find nearby EV charging stations. It also allows users to view station details, check availability, and book charging slots online.
2. Who can use this EV charging application?
Any person who owns or uses an electric vehicle can use this application. It is useful for personal vehicle owners, taxi drivers, and fleet operators.
3. Is this application free to use?
Yes, searching for stations and checking availability is completely free. Only charging services at stations may have real-world costs, depending on the station.
4. Do I need to create an account to use the app?
For basic features like searching stations, login is not mandatory. However, booking slots and giving reviews may require entering basic user details.
5. Which locations or cities are supported?
The app currently supports selected major cities. More cities and locations can be added easily in future updates.
6. How does the app find nearby EV charging stations?
The app uses city or area input provided by the user. It displays stations on a map using stored location data and map integration.
7. Can I search stations by city or area name?
Yes, users can search stations by entering city names, areas, or station names. This makes it easy to locate chargers even in unfamiliar locations.
8. Does the app show stations on a map?
Yes, the app uses map integration to show station locations visually. This helps users understand distance, direction, and nearby landmarks.
9. What do the different station colors indicate?
Green color indicates that the station is available for charging. Red shows that the station is busy, and grey indicates offline or unavailable stations.
10. Can I see full station details before booking?
Yes, every station card shows important information like charger type and speed. This helps users make better decisions before booking a slot.
11. What types of EV chargers are supported?
The app supports common charger types such as CCS2, Type 2, and CHAdeMO. Users can choose the connector that matches their vehicle.

You can check your vehicle manual or manufacturer website. Then select the same connector type while searching or booking.
